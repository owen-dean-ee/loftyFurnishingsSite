Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

At Lofty Furnishing, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and process your personal data when you book a service, contact us, or visit https://loftyfurnishing.com (the “Site”).

1. Information We Collect

When you make a booking, purchase a loft bed service, or submit an application on our Site, we collect personal information you provide, including:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Dorm delivery address and room number
  • Billing and transaction details (processed securely via Stripe)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to coordinate, schedule, and complete the installation and delivery of loft beds to your room at Penn State University, to process payment securely, and to contact you with service updates.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your contact and booking data are shared only with our installation crews to facilitate delivery. Financial data is shared directly with Stripe (our payment gateway) and is never stored on our servers.

4. Contact Us

For any questions about your privacy, or if you would like to request that we delete your personal information, please contact us at support@loftyfurnishing.com.
