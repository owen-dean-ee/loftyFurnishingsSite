Our Story

We know that moving into college can feel overwhelming—especially on move-in day. At Lofty Furnishing, our goal is to make that first day in your dorm as smooth as possible.

Founded by PSU students with one simple idea: collegiate dorms are cramped, and packing/building furniture is a logistical nightmare. We designed custom, heavy-duty premium cedar loft beds that safely maximize under-bed spacing (providing a full 38″ of vertical clearance for desks, couches, or dressers).

White-Glove Installation

Unlike flat-pack furniture you have to assemble yourself after a long road trip to State College, Lofty Furnishing handles everything. Our local crews schedule delivery times in precise 1-hour slots, and we fully install your loft frame in under 30 minutes.

Sustainable Cedar Craftsmanship

Our frames are handcrafted from sustainable cedar, left unstained or stained in rich finishes like Espresso or English Chestnut. Natural cedar is exceptionally strong, smells wonderful, and looks beautiful, creating a warm, premium aesthetic for your bedroom space.
